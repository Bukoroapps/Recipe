Hash brown ham and egg nests

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 50 mins
Makes:6 (or makes 12)

01.

Preheat oven to 180°C fan forced. Grease a 12-hole non-stick (1/3 cup-capacity) muffin pan.

02.

Heat a large, lightly greased non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Cook ham for 4 minutes or until golden and crisp. Drain on paper towel.

03.

Place peeled and grated potato in a colander and squeeze out all moisture. Transfer to a large bowl and add oil, parmesan and half the ham. Season with salt and pepper.

04.

Divide the potato mixture into each muffin hole, pressing into base and up the sides with the back of a spoon.

05.

Bake for 20–25 minutes. Crack an egg into each hash brown cup one and bake for a further 8–10 minutes or egg whites are cooked and egg yolk is just setting.

06.

Scatter hash brown egg cups with chives, parmesan and remaining ham.

Chilaquiles Verde

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serving: 2

01.

Preheat oven to 200°C

02.

In a medium bowl, combine tomato pickle and chipotle bean salsa

03.

In an oven proof 30cm frypan, stir in combined sauces over medium to medium high heat. Once bubbling, take off heat, add the chips, then scatter chickpeas and feta

04.

Bake for 10 mins, or until hot and cheese has slightly softened

05.

Meanwhile, in a non-stick fry pan, fry two eggs until cooked, but still runny

06.

Remove pan from oven and top with the fried eggs, avocado and fresh salsa

Tinolang Tahong

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serving: 3

01.

Heat a cooking pot and pour-in cooking oil.

02.

Sauce garlic and onion.

03.

Add ginger and mussels, and then cook for a minute.

04.

Pour-in water. let boil. Cook for 5 minutes.

05.

06.

07.

Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve. Share and enjoy.

Green Eggs & Ham

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serving: 4

01.

Place eggs, 100g spinach and parmesan into a blender and blend until combined. Season with salt and pepper.

02.

Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat until hot. Add the oil and swirl to coat pan base. Add egg mixture and cook for 1½ minutes or until mixture starts to set. Using a spatula, push set egg towards centre of pan, tilting pan to allow uncooked egg to run over base. Cook for 2 minutes or until eggs form creamy curds.

03.

Top bun base with spinach leaves, ham and green eggs. Sprinkle with parmesan, top with bun lids to serve
