Hash brown ham and egg nests

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 50 mins
Makes:6 (or makes 12)

01.

Preheat oven to 180°C fan forced. Grease a 12-hole non-stick (1/3 cup-capacity) muffin pan.

02.

Heat a large, lightly greased non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Cook ham for 4 minutes or until golden and crisp. Drain on paper towel.

03.

Place peeled and grated potato in a colander and squeeze out all moisture. Transfer to a large bowl and add oil, parmesan and half the ham. Season with salt and pepper.

04.

Divide the potato mixture into each muffin hole, pressing into base and up the sides with the back of a spoon.

05.

Bake for 20–25 minutes. Crack an egg into each hash brown cup one and bake for a further 8–10 minutes or egg whites are cooked and egg yolk is just setting.

06.

Scatter hash brown egg cups with chives, parmesan and remaining ham.

Chilaquiles Verde

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serving: 2

01.

Preheat oven to 200°C

02.

In a medium bowl, combine tomato pickle and chipotle bean salsa

03.

In an oven proof 30cm frypan, stir in combined sauces over medium to medium high heat. Once bubbling, take off heat, add the chips, then scatter chickpeas and feta

04.

Bake for 10 mins, or until hot and cheese has slightly softened

05.

Meanwhile, in a non-stick fry pan, fry two eggs until cooked, but still runny

06.

Remove pan from oven and top with the fried eggs, avocado and fresh salsa

Green Eggs & Ham

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Serving: 4

01.

Place eggs, 100g spinach and parmesan into a blender and blend until combined. Season with salt and pepper.

02.

Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat until hot. Add the oil and swirl to coat pan base. Add egg mixture and cook for 1½ minutes or until mixture starts to set. Using a spatula, push set egg towards centre of pan, tilting pan to allow uncooked egg to run over base. Cook for 2 minutes or until eggs form creamy curds.

03.

Top bun base with spinach leaves, ham and green eggs. Sprinkle with parmesan, top with bun lids to serve

Egg Florentine Breakfast Bake

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 40 mins
Serving: 6-8

01.

Preheat oven to 180°C/160°C fan-forced. Lightly spray a 20 x 30cm (base measurement) baking tin with oil and line with baking paper.

02.

Heat oil in a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook onion, stirring, for 3-4 minutes or until softened. Add garlic and mushrooms and cook, stirring, for 3-4 minutes or until golden. Add spinach and stir until just wilted. Set aside to cool slightly.

03.

Whisk eggs, milk and half the parmesan together in a large bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Spread mushroom mixture over base of prepared dish. Pour over egg mixture and evenly distribute vegetables. Top with halved cherry tomatoes cut side up and sprinkle with remaining parmesan.

04.

Bake for 30 minutes or until golden and set. Let stand for 10 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature scattered with basil.

Overnight Summer Oats With Roasted Nectarines

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Serving: 4

01.

Roughly chop 1/3 cup almonds and place into a bowl. Add rolled oats, apple, almond milk, sultanas and currants. Stir until combined. Cover and refrigerate overnight

02.

Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan forced. Line a baking tray with baking paper. Combine nectarine wedges and maple syrup in a bowl. Spread on prepared baking tray and bake for 15 minutes or until nectarines are golden and softened. Spread remaining almonds on a second tray and bake for the last 5 minutes of cooking

03.

Stir extra milk into porridge, if you like, and spoon into serving bowls. Top with roasted nectarines and almonds. Drizzle with extra maple syrup

Buttermilk Waffles

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 2 mins
Serving: 3

01.

Preheat waffle maker.

02.

Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl starting with flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well using a wire whisk.

Hotsilog (Hotdog Sinangang at Itlog)

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serving: 1

01.

Pour water in a small cooking pot. Let boil.

02.

Add hotdogs and then pour 2 tablespoons cooking oil. Continue to cook until the water evaporates.

03.

Once the water is gone, fry the hotdogs in the remaining oil for 1 to 2 minutes while slowly rolling it back and forth to prevent it from getting burnt. Set aside.

04.

Prepare the eggs by heating 4 tablespoons of cooking oil in a pan.

05.

Once the oil gets hot, crack a piece of egg and start to fry it. As the egg is frying, grab a spoon and scoop the oil from the bottom of the pot. Pour the oil over the egg. Do this until the yolk gets a white covering. Remove the egg and set aside. Do the same step on the other piece of egg.

06.

Arrange the garlic fried rice, hotdogs, and fried eggs in a plate. Put the slices of tomato on the side.
