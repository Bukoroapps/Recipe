Bistek Tagalog (beefsteak)

Prep: 60 mins
Cook: 40 mins
Serving: 5

01.

Marinate beef in soy sauce, lemon (or calamansi), and ground black pepper for at least 1 hour. Note: marinate overnight for best result

02.

Heat the cooking oil in a pan then pan-fry half of the onions until the texture becomes soft. Set aside

03.

Drain the marinade from the beef. Set it aside. Pan-fry the beef on the same pan where the onions were fried for 1 minute per side. Remove from the pan. Set aside

04.

Add more oil if needed. Saute garlic and remaining raw onions until onion softens.

05.

Pour the remaining marinade and water. Bring to a boil.

06.

Add beef. Cover the pan and simmer until meat is tender. Note: Add water as needed..

07.

Season with ground black pepper and salt as needed. Top with pan-fried onions.

08.

Transfer to a serving plate. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!

Chicken Adobo

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 35 mins
Serving: 4

01.

Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Mix well. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour. Note: the longer the time, the better

02.

Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil.

03.

When the oil is hot enough, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.

04.

Pour-in the remaining marinade, including garlic. Add water. Bring to a boil

05.

Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken gets tender

06.

Add vinegar. Stir and cook for 10 minutes.

07.

Put-in the sugar, and salt. Stir and turn the heat off.Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!

Homemade Beef Tapa

Prep: 12 hrs
Cook: 15 mins
Serving: 4

01.

In a container, combine soy sauce, garlic, salt, pepper, and sugar and mix well. Set aside

02.

Place the beef in the clear plastic bag

03.

Pour-in the the mixed seasonings in the clear plastic bag with meat and mix well

04.

Place inside the refrigerator and marinate for a minimum of 12 hours

05.

In a pan, place 1 cup water and bring to a boil

06.

Add 3 tbsp of cooking oil

07.

Put-in the marinated beef tapa and cook until the water evaporates.
